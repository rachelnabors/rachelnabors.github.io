Mozilla approached me about building an interactive site to celebrate the new visual design tools in Firefox Developer Edition 44. With only five weeks until launch, I had to come up with an arresting idea fast, so I drew from my childhood fascination with the deep sea to pitch a site full of dev tools challenges with fascinating deep sea creatures.

Production had to move fast, so I requested muscle: Mozilla put two JavaScript developers on the project and a music designer. I brought in a logo designer, a local illustrator, and a copy editor to round out the team. And yes, I rolled up my sleeves and coded a great deal of the front end as well. We made the deadline—and made something unforgettable.

For me, DevTools Challenger is proof that when we work together, we can make something greater than ourselves.
