Back in 2015 I started curating and circulating Web Animation Weekly. In a little over a year its readership grew to 3,000 subscribers, and I brought on a features editor to help shoulder the load. Some readers donate money to help cover the costs of publication, but otherwise it is labor of love.

The next year, 2016, in keeping with my theme of gardening over gatekeeping, with the help of a few volunteers from the Web Animation Weekly community, we set up the Animation at Work Slack and a form that lets people add themselves to the Slack.

Neither of these projects would be as far along as they are without the help of volunteers and the strong backing of the community. For that I am proud.
